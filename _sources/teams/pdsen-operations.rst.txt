PDSEN Operations Team
=====================

The PDS Engineering Node Operations Team is responsible for managing pds.nasa.gov, facilitating data releases across the PDS, and many, many other day-to-day processes needed for the PDS to run smoothly.

* Scripts and Software: https://github.com/NASA-PDS/pdsen-operations
* Issue Tracking: https://github.com/NASA-PDS/pdsen-operations/issues
* Wiki: https://github.com/NASA-PDS/pdsen-operations/wiki
* Github Teams: @NASA-PDS/pdsen-operations and @pds-data-dictionaries/pdsen-operations
