Software Changes¶

The changes types are ‘Bug’, ‘Enhancement’ or ‘Requirement’. For each software repository, the changes are listed in 2 categories:

  • Planned Updates

  • Other Updates

The ‘Planned Updates’ are organized by ‘Themes’ (or ‘Release Themes’), which are defined in advance and approved by the PDS Software Working Group (see `Plan B12.1-SNAPSHOT`_’) The ‘Other Updates’ occurs during the build cycle witout being planned or attached to a theme. They are organized by types (bug, enhancements, requirements…). Any updates that require a de-scope of planned tasks are reviewed by the PDS Software Working Group.

The deliveries are validated by the development team and go through an additional Integration & Test process, as applicable, as indicated by a specific icon in the following tables.
