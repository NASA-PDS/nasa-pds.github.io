For each software repository, the changes are listed in 2 categories:

  • Planned Updates

The ‚ÄòPlanned Updates‚Äô are organized by ‚ÄòThemes‚Äô (or ‚ÄòRelease Themes‚Äô), which are defined in advance and approved by the PDS Software Working Group (see Plan B14.0).

The ‚ÄòOther Updates‚Äô occurs during the build cycle without being planned or attached to a theme. They are organized by types (bug, enhancements, requirements, tasks). Any updates that require a de-scope of planned tasks are reviewed by the PDS Software Working Group.
